The "Immersive Journey" Structure

A cinematic flow designed for the Dark Nebula aesthetic

Proposed Page Flow
Section 01

The Cosmic Hero

A full-height (100vh) immersive entry. The background is a deep space nebula (dynamic canvas or heavy CSS gradients). The content is centered and minimal: just your name, the core "AI Engineering" signal, and a primary CTA that pulses.

Section 02

The HUD Metrics (Horizontal Scroll)

As you scroll, the hero text fades, and a horizontal "HUD" bar slides in. It shows your "Active Vectors" (LLMs, MLOps, etc.) using glowing progress bars and micro-animations. This establishes technical authority immediately.

Section 03

The Research Ledger (Featured Writing)

Large, high-contrast glass cards for your blog posts. We use "Depth of Field" effects (blurring background cards) to focus the eye on the featured item. Hovering "activates" the card with a light-sweep effect.

Section 04

The Academic Node Map

A visual representation of your academic work. Instead of a simple list, we use a "node" layout where research items are connected, showing the progression of your learning.

Section 05

The Signal Hub (Footer/Connect)

A deep-bottom section that brings back the nebula background. Contains a minimal contact form and social signals.

Structural Logic

Visual Hook (S1)

Attracts the audience within the first 3 seconds with a "cinematic" feel.

Technical Proof (S2)

Instantly proves credibility before they read a single word of content.

High-Value Content (S3-S4)

Deep-dives presented as "premium" items worth their time.
