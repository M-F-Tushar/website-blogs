Design Section: Theme & Core Styling

Defining the "Dark Nebula" foundation

1. Color Palette & Background

Background Strategy

A deep base of #020617 (Slate-950) with animated mesh gradients (nebulas) in #1d4ed8 (Blue-700) and #7e22ce (Purple-700).

Glass Properties

Cards will use backdrop-filter: blur(40px) with a very thin 1px border of rgba(255,255,255,0.08) and a subtle inner shadow.

2. Typography & Motion

Typography

Headings: A bold, wide sans-serif (e.g., Clash Display or Inter Tight) with tight letter-spacing (-0.05em).

Body: High-legibility Inter with increased line-height (1.8) for long-form reading.

Motion (Framer Motion)

Scroll-Linked Animations: Sections fade and scale slightly as they enter the viewport.

Hover Interactions: Glass cards "glow" and lift slightly on hover using spring physics.

3. Technical Implementation

Our Stack

  • Tailwind CSS 4: Utilizing the new JIT engine for ultra-precise gradient and blur control.
  • Framer Motion: For smooth, hardware-accelerated transitions and scroll effects.
  • Lucide React: For crisp, technical-looking icons.
