Design Direction Comparison

How do you want the platform to "feel"?

A

Current: Futuristic Glassmorphism

High use of transparency, blurs, and cyan gradients. Feels like a sci-fi interface.

Pros

  • Highly interactive and modern
  • Visually unique

Cons

  • Can feel "busy" or overwhelming
  • Text readability can suffer over complex backgrounds
B

Proposed: Refined Technical (Minimalist)

Clean typography, generous whitespace, and a high-contrast palette (Dark Mode first or Clean Light). Focuses on "Documentarian" clarity.

Pros

  • Focuses purely on content and code
  • Feels authoritative and professional

Cons

  • Might feel "colder" or less flashy
  • Relies heavily on perfect typography

What are we optimizing for?

Technical Credibility

The audience should trust the engineering depth presented.

Reading Experience

Deep dives into AI/ML need to be comfortable to read for 10+ minutes.

Navigation Velocity

Finding specific research notes or project details should be instant.
